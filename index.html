About me

Greetings! My name is Dillon Voravongsy, as you can see from the heading. I am Asian American borned and raised in the Twin Cities. I graduated highschool in 2018 from Shakopee Highschool, and went to working full time. 2 years later, I am now commiting to a career that interest me. When I am not working, I like to go bowling, try new things, and be adventurious. I also have an interest in collecting shoes and limited clothing then reselling pairs for profit. I've been doing this for a few years and I enjoy it. Music is one thing in my life that keeps me brings me back to Earth when things are getting stressful. For me, artists such as Kendrick Lamar, Juice Wrld, Mac Miller, Frank Ocean, Kid Cudi, are good for helping me relax and have a deeper meaning in topics.
